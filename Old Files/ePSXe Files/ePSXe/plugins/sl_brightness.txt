Pete's OpenGL2 GLslang shader effect: "Brightness"
==================================================

Description:
------------
A shader for increasing the screen brightness, depending on the configured "Shader effect level".

Installation:
-------------
Copy the "gpuPeteOGL2.slv" and "gpuPeteOGL2.slf" files into a emu sub-directory called "shaders". Configure the plugin's "Shader effects" option to "5 - GLSlang files". The "Shader effect level" option is controlling the brightness:

Level 1: original colors
Level 2: somewhat brighter
Level 3: even more brighter
Level 4: brightest
